General infomation

Thank you for taking the time to get involved with selecting the officers for next year! You can vote for SWE's executive board for 2015-2016 year down below. You can also get involved by applying for an officer position next year. More information is below.

All officer position descriptions and responsibilities can be found here.

Apply to be a Fall 2015 Officer

If you would like to get more involved with SWE beyond a general or committee member, apply to be an officer next year! Applications are open until Monday, March 30th at 5pm.

Officer position descriptions and responsibilities can be found here. Officer positions will now be a semesterly commitment.

No prior officer experience necessary. No formal elections and speeches as with the executive positions. Callback interviews may occur on a need basis.

Executive Board Elections
