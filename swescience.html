SWE Science


SWE Science Saturdays and SWE Science Scholars are a series of activities hosted once every month that teach engineering ideas and principles to 4th and 5th grade students and 6th - 8th grade students. The students work in groups to complete different hands-on projects using their creativity and knowledge. Some examples of previous activities include creating catapults and building “earthquake-proof” structures out of classroom materials such as popsicle sticks, rubber bands, straws, and chopsticks.

Our members lead two hour hands-on engineering lessons and challenges on a Saturday once a month from 10 a.m. to 12 p.m. Registration is conducted on a monthly basis and registration for the upcoming SWE Science event opens a few days after the preceding event. Spaces are given to those who register on a first come first serve basis!


If you have any questions about this program, please contact us by email at swesciencesaturday@gmail.com and swesciencescholars@gmail.com, respectively.
