Overview

"While you are at SWE, your triumphs will earn you house points, while any rule-breaking will lose house points. At the end of the year, the house with the most points is awarded the house cup, a great honour."

  1. 4 houses
  2. Earn points: attending events, hanging out with your family while taking selfies, being active in a committee
  3. Your house will be put to the test at House Competitions
  4. At the end of the year there will be a house cup awarded to the house with the most points
  5. We will also have prizes for the top point earners at the end of each semester. Top point earners in each house will receive recognition at our GMs :)
  6. Houses and Committees are two entirely separate entities. However we strongly encourage SWE members to partake in both.
  7. If members are VERY adamant about switching into a particular group, they must contact the Membership Director directly. This procedure can be done anytime during the semester unless it's 30 days before semester award ceremony
  8. Houses will stay the same for an entire academic year.
  9. The Head of House should be your main point of contact for any official House business. However any officer in your house can be a mentor.
