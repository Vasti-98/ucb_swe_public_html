Overnight Host Program


The New Admit Overnight Host Program (OHP) is a unique event specifically for women admitted to UC Berkeleyâ€™s College of Engineering and Chemical Engineering majors from the College of Chemistry. The program provides female admits an opportunity to visit the Cal campus, interact with current engineering students, interact with research laboratories and alumni, attend a banquet with UC Berkeley faculty and professors, and sleep overnight in student housing in order to experience life as a Berkeley Engineer.




We host OHP each year. However, due to the cancellation of campus events, we will be providing an online version of this program. SWE is working hard to provide the same content that you would have received if you had attended OHP in-person.

This will include recordings of campus/dorm tours, a professor lecture, alumni statements, student perspectives, lightning talks from current engineering students and several student panels via Zoom. In addition, we are organizing a pen pal system to connect admits to current engineering students. Sign up by April 16th here if you are interested!



The content and resources mentioned above will be provided and updated in the coming weeks on the social media platforms below and our website!
