Professional Development Month



Professional Development Month (PDM) is an annual series of career workshops throughout October for students interested in expanding their professional development. Every year, the UC Berkeley Society of Women Engineers invites companies like Microsoft, PwC, and Slack to host educational seminars throughout the month.
