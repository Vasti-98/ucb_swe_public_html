New Admit Overnight Host Program


The New Admit Overnight Host Program (OHP) is a unique event designed specifically for women admitted to UC Berkeley’s College of Engineering and the Chemical Engineering major in the College of Chemistry for Fall 2018. The program provides female admits the opportunity to visit the Cal campus, interact with engineering student organizations and research laboratories, attend a banquet with UC Berkeley faculty and professors, and sleep overnight in student housing in order to experience life as a Berkeley engineer. This year’s program takes place from April 20th-21st, 2018. Participants are also encouraged to attend Cal Day on April 21st.

All newly-admitted women to UC Berkeley’s engineering programs will receive an invitation to the New Admit Overnight Host Program. This program is provided free of cost to all invited students. Invitations are sent out the last week in March.Please email Haruka Ichikawa and Louise Feng at ucb.swe.admit@gmail.com with any questions about the program.

Registration for the 2018 New Admit Overnight Host Program is now open until April 10th, 2018. Registration will be accepted on a first-come, first-serve basis. After submitting the registration form, students must complete a Waiver of Liability Form AND a Medical Authorization Form. These forms must be scanned and emailed to ucb.swe.admit@gmail.com by April 18, 2018 at 11:59 PM. Failure to do so may result in your spot being given away to someone on the waitlist.

Parents/Guardians - Want to know more about Berkeley Engineering and UC Berkeley? There is a special program just for you on Friday, April 20th from 9am-4pm (no overnight accommodations provided). Cost for parents is $20 per person and includes a light breakfast and lunch. We will cover topics including: Housing, Financial Aid, Safety, Mental Health and Wellness, Academic Advising and much more. Learn more about the day's agenda and register on the Engineering Student Services website: https://engineering.berkeley.edu/sweohp.
