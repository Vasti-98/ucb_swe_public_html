New Admit Overnight Host Program


The New Admit Overnight Host Program (OHP) is a unique event designed specifically for women admitted to UC Berkeley’s College of Engineering for Fall 2017. The program provides female admits the opportunity to visit the Cal campus, interact with engineering student organizations and research laboratories, attend a banquet with UC Berkeley faculty and professors, and sleep overnight in student housing in order to experience life as a Berkeley engineer. This year’s program takes place from April 20th-21st, 2018. Participants are also encouraged to attend Cal Day on April 22st.

All newly-admitted women to UC Berkeley’s engineering programs will receive an invitation to the New Admit Overnight Host Program. This program is provided free of cost to all invited students. Invitations are sent out the last week in March.

In addition to the New Admit Overnight Host Program, UC Berkeley’s Engineering Student Services offers a one-day event for the parents of admitted female engineering students, which will take place on Friday, April 20th, 2018.

More information regarding both of these programs will be posted closer to their event dates.
