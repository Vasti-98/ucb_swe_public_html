New Admit Overnight Host Program

The New Admit Overnight Host Program (OHP) is a unique event designed specifically for women admitted to UC Berkeley’s College of Engineering for Fall 2017. The program provides female admits the opportunity to visit the Cal campus, interact with engineering student organizations and research laboratories, attend a banquet with UC Berkeley faculty and professors, and sleep overnight in student housing in order to experience life as a Berkeley engineer. This year’s program takes place from April 21st-22nd, 2017. Participants are also encouraged to attend Cal Day on April 22nd.

All newly-admitted women to UC Berkeley’s engineering programs will receive an invitation to the New Admit Overnight Host Program. This program is provided free of cost to all students invited. Invitations are sent out the last week in March.


In addition to the New Admit Overnight Host Program, UC Berkeley’s Engineering Student Services offers a one-day event for the parents of admitted female engineering students, which will take place on Friday, April 21st, 2017 from 9am-3pm.

More information about both of these programs will be posted closer to their event dates.
