OHP ucb.swe.admit@gmail.com

Chair:

The Overnight Host Program is one of the most exciting events in the spring; partnering with ESS (Engineering Student Services) we invite new admits to stay overnight during Cal Day weekend to gain a first-hand view of what Berkeley can offer. We all remember college-decision time...and every one of you chose Berkeley! Join OHP and help newly admitted female engineers make their decisions by providing them with an amazing experience they can't forget! EVERYONE is welcome to apply!

Public Relations ucb.swe.pr@gmail.com

Chairs: Alicia Audong, Vy Luu

The Public Relations committee is in charge of keeping SWE members in the loop about SWE events and opportunities. The main ways in which we do this are through preparing and distributing the weekly SWEmail, producing the newsletter that is distributed at general meetings, flyering around campus, chalking messages in classrooms, and posting social media updates including Facebook and Twitter. If you have a passion for writing, photography, design, communications/social media, or just an interest in public relations then we would love to have you join our team! (No experience necessary)

Shadow an Engineer

Chairs: Cindy Wong, Shanaaz Deo

The Shadow an Engineer Program provides undergraduate engineering students with externships over winter break to allow them the chance to see an inside peak of what it is like to be a full-time engineer. Develop your formal communication skills by directly contacting companies and other students to inform them of the event and answer all their questions! We're looking for students who are interested in filling the roles of Corporate Relations, Student Relations, Public Relations in our committee. Please apply to join!

Professional Development Week (PDM)

Chair:

SWE is hosting the third annual Professional Development Month (PDM) this fall! PDM is a month-long series of events with members of industry to equip engineering students with the knowledge and tools to secure a job or internship. The events will focus on cruising a career fair, acing the interview, and more! All events will be served with food. PDM is currently putting together a committee of 8 day chairs and 2 publicists to bring the event together. If interested, talk to Francesca Ledesma or Aly Scheske at SWE GM1 or apply here: https://goo.gl/forms/MgDLS5s1Q62T9Pel1.
