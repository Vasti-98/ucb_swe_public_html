How will I know if I am accepted into the program?

You will receive an email within a week of registration that will indicate whether you are accepted into the program or are on the waitlist. Sign up early to ensure you receive a spot!


Do I need to fill out emergency forms?

In order to participate in the Overnight Host Program, students must fill out two forms, a waiver of liability form and a medical treatment form. Forms can be scanned and emailed to ucb.swe.admit@gmail.com or mailed to:


Society of Women Engineers
230 Bechtel Engineering Center, #1702
Berkeley, CA 94720

Forms must be received by April 19th, 2017.


What do I need to bring?

In addition to your standard toiletries and overnight supplies, please bring the following:

  • A sleeping bag/pad/pillow
    • Most of our hosts do not have extra beds in their rooms - come prepared to sleep on a floor or couch
  • A formal or semi-formal outfit
    • For the dinner banquet
  • Comfortable clothes for both Friday and Saturday (and lots of sunscreen!)
  • Optional: a small knapsack or bag to carry day-belongings around.
    • We will be storing your luggage in a secure location for the duration of the day. If you would like to carry around a water bottle or wallet, or have someplace to store flyers and information packets, you may find a small bag useful.

Where/when do I check in/check out?

Check-in will be on Friday, April 21st, 9:00am - 10:00am in the Garbarini Lounge (second floor of Bechtel Engineering Center). Bring all sleeping bags and luggage to the check-in desk. We will store your belongings in a secure location until it is time to meet your overnight host.


Check-out will be be on Saturday, April 22nd, 9am - 4:00pm in 240 Bechtel Engineering Center. You will bring your sleeping bags and luggage to breakfast and we will store your belongings while you attend Cal Day. Return anytime to 240 Bechtel before 4:00 PM to have a parent sign you out (if you are under 18) and to collect your belongings. Note: we ask that you complete a quick survey before you sign out.
