Volunteer

We are currently searching for volunteers to help with the Society of Women Engineers’ Overnight Host Program (OHP).


Volunteering takes place on Friday, April 21st, from 8AM-10:30PM, and on Friday, April 22nd, from 9AM-5PM.


As a volunteer, you’ll:

  • Lead lab tours, scavenger hunts, and evening activities
  • Answer questions about your experiences as a Cal Student (questions may involve your major, extracurricular activities, living situation, etc.)
  • Give insider’s advice on how to survive Berkeley Engineering!
  • And more!

Please note: You do not have to be a female student to volunteer for OHP!
