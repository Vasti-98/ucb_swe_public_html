About Us

Supporting both students and professionals, SWE consists of over 37,000 members in 100 professional sections and 300 student sections. The UC Berkeley student chapter has been established since 1975.


The UC Berkeley SWE section supports students through professional development, social events and outreach to young women interested in the STEM field.
