SWE Science Scholars

SWE Science Scholars are 2 hour fun engineering projects and lessons from 10am-12pm for local middle school students. This is a continuation program for SWE Science Saturdays. Spaces are given to those who RSVP first!

Each confirmed participant must make a $2 deposit for each event to cover the cost of supplies. In your sign-up form you will designate whether you want your deposit returned or donated to SWE. Deposits will only be returned upon attending the designated activity.

Sign-up:

  • Your child must be in 6th, 7th, or 8th grade (if younger - please see SWE Science Saturdays)
  • Sign Up by filling out the SWE Science Scholars Form.
  • Print and sign the Photo Consent Release Form. If you do not have a printer, we will have extra copies at the activity.
  • A confirmation email will be sent to you the week of the event with the room location UC Berkeley Campus.
  • If your child cannot attend an event for which he or she is already confirmed, please cancel your registration for that SWE Science Scholar.
