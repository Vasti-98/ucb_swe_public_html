Outreach General Information

To volunteer for any of our outreach events please fill out this volunteer form.

The Society of Women Engineers has a number of outreach events:

  • SWE Science Saturdays — 4th and 5th grade
  • SWE Science Scholars — middle school
  • Mini University (Mini-U)
  • Junior Solar Sprint Challenge
  • Have No Fear Engineer

We also have a number of events in collaboration with other groups:

  • Girls Go Tech
  • Expanding Your Horizons, Mills & SF (EYH)
  • Engineering 4 Kids Day (E4k)
  • YES Day
