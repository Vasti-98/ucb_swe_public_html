SWE Science Saturdays

Attention returning SWE Science Saturday Parents: Welcome back! The sign up process has been changed from previous years. It is described below, with major changes in BOLD.

SWE Science Saturdays are 2 hour fun engineering lessons and challenges from 10am-12pm for 4th and 5th graders from local elementary schools. Spaces are given to those who register on a first come first serve basis!

A $2 dollar donation is suggested but not required from students to cover the cost of supplies. In contrast to previous years, we are no longer using PayPal for donations, so this donation would be in-person at the event should you choose to give one.

**Note: In the past, we have been fairly lenient about letting students who are not registered for a class attend because our classes were smaller and we love doing science experiments with your students! However, safety is our first priority, and as a result we are no longer able to accommodate students who are not registered. Having more students than we planned for presents a risk because we do not have enough volunteers to supervise all your students and make sure they are accounted for and safe. Thank you!

Sign-up:

  1. Your child must be in 4th or 5th grade (if older - please see SWE Science Scholars)
  2. Please note that the registration process has changed from previous years. We will now be doing monthly pre-registrations in order to maximize the number of students who are able to participate in our classes. Registration for the event begins the Monday after the previous month's SWE Science Saturday at 5 PM PST. Please fill out the SWE Science Saturdays form ASAP to ensure that your child has a chance to participate. These are the dates for the Spring 2016 Semester of SWE Science Saturdays:

    Please note: In contrast to previous years, filling out the SWE Science Saturday Form does not necessarily mean you are registered for the class. You will receive an email confirmation the Monday before the SWE Science event informing you as to whether you have gotten into the class, are waitlisted, or if the class is all full.
  3. Fill out the Medical Waiver Form and Waiver of Liability. Please send scanned copies of both documents to . or return them to us on the day of the event.
  4. A confirmation email will be sent to you with details as to whether you are registered or waitlisted for a class, where the class will take place, and any additional information such as the room location UC Berkeley Campus.
  5. If your child cannot attend an event for which he or she is already confirmed, please cancel your registration for that SWE Science Saturday by emailing us as soon as possible, so another student can take your student's place at SWE Science Saturday.
